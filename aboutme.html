About Me

I grew up playing Contra, Double Dragon, and Super Mario Bros on the Famicom, and have had a passion for video games for as long as I can remember. I decided to pursue a career in the video games industry when I enrolled at National University of Singapore.

I believe in creating creative, imaginative games that aren't afraid to be weird. I am most inspired by games like Monster Hunter, Dragon's Dogma, GRIS and NieR, that have strong identities. However, I also appreciate light and casual "lunch break" games that can be played in short sessions.

I enjoy expressing myself through the arts and practise DJing, dance, and roller skating when I'm not gaming in my free time. I also love to eat and can cook up a mean steak on the grill or the stove.
