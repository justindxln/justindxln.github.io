Bill.eGoat


Bill.eGoat was a bill processing web application that allowed the user to conveniently track their spendings. The user could upload their bill to the application, where relevant information like company name, due date, and due amount would be automatically processed and added to the database. The breakdown and analysis of the collated data could then be displayed in graph format.

NUS Forum


NUSForum was a reimagining of the students' discussion board for the National University of Singapore. Students could ask module-related questions, and tutors, professors, and other students of the same module could discuss or answer them. It featured an upvote system, and tutors and professors could highlight and pin-to-top answers they chose.
