Best Blocks [Unity]

App Store Link


Role: Engineer
Best Blocks is a shape puzzle game where players forms full horizontal or vertical lines by fitting puzzle pieces on the board, at which point the line is cleared from the board, similar to Tetris. The player has to complete specific tasks each level like form a number of horizontal or vertical lines, or clearing specific tiles.


Word Forest [Unity]

App Store Link


Role: Engineer
Word Forest is a word guessing game that uses the basic mechanics of Word Collect with a Forest building progression system. As secondary currencies are earned through completing levels accurately, they can be used to plant trees on an empty landscape. As trees fill up the space, structures and animals will appear until the landscape is full and the player will move on to the next forest.

Word Tropics [Unity]

App Store Link


Role: Engineer
Word Tropics is a crossword puzzle game where the player has to fill up an empty crossboard by slotting in words from a set of words given. As levels get more difficult, more words per levels are introduced including extra redundant words.


Word Collect [Unity]

App Store Link


Role: Engineer
Word Collect is a vocabulary game where players complete levels by finding as many words as possible from the given combination of letters. Consecutive correct answers build up combos to grant extra rewards. Special challenge levels with a higher skill ceiling rotate daily and give special rewards based on how well players can guess specific words.

Blood Collector [Unreal Engine 4]


Role: Designer, Programmer
Blood Collector has the player assume the role of a vampire, who goes out nightly to collect blood from human civillians, while fighting or avoiding vampire hunters. The resource management aspect of the game is challenging as the player can choose to save up all the blood collected to meet a set quota to progress the game or spend it on upgrading the character to collect more blood.


Acceleration [Unreal Engine 4]


Solo Project
Acceleration is a spin on simple obstacle-avoidance gameplay by putting the difficult in the controls. The player controls the acceleration on the object instead of direct movement, hence they have to think ahead instead of relying on twitch reflex. Additional obstacle types that slows down or hunts the player also appears as the game progresses.


Collision [Unreal Engine 4]


Solo Project
Collision plays around with the concept of harmful obstacles usually seen in top-down shooter or platforming games. The moving blades alternate between being harmful obstalces that damage the player or helpful objects that restore the player's shield. The player's goal is to avoid the harmful obstacles while collecting gold-coloured orbs that appear on the playing field.
