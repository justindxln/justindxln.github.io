Endless Runner Implementation Details (In-Progress)

Gameplay Systems

Player Status Manager

This class manages all non-movement features related to the Player Character, including HitPoint management, buffs, and status effects

HUD

Player Status HUD

This HUD section shows the player Health, Shield, and Double Jump status. The Health bar color is dynamic and depends on the player's curren health percentage. The Shield bar envelops the health bar instead of being a separate bar, making it more intuitive for displaying the shield amount. The Double Jump indicator is a mini progress bar to show the cooldown progress, with an outer glow to clearly indicate whether Double Jump is ready.

Score HUD

The Score HUD displays the score multiplier and the current accummulated score. The multiplier has dynamic color and animation speed depending on how close the player is to the moving wall. The multiplier text also has a small "pop" animation whenever it changes value
